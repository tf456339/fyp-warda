Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is it compulsory to mention my bank account details

No, these details are not required if you are going to purchase a book. However, these details are necessary for all the sellers so that we can transfer the amount of sold books.

Is it compulsory to mention my address details

No, these details are not required if you are selling your books. However, these details are necessary for all the buyers so that we can deliever the purchase books on your address.

Who will be responsible for any scams or faulty orders?

Admin will hold the payment and will only release after the confirmation from buyer. Alternatively, buyer can return the book to the admin if it does not matches the entered description by seller.
